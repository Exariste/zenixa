Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a website project take?
+
Typically 2–4 weeks for a standard website, depending on complexity and content. We provide a detailed timeline before starting.
Do you provide website maintenance after launch?
+
Yes! All our plans include post-launch support. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your website updated and secure.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
+
Absolutely. Every website we build is 100% responsive and optimized for all devices — mobile, tablet, and desktop.
Do you provide SEO services with web design?
+
Yes, all our websites are built with SEO best practices. We also offer dedicated SEO packages for ongoing optimization.
Can I update the website content myself?
+
Yes! We can integrate a CMS (Content Management System) that lets you easily update text, images, and content without any coding knowledge.
What information do you need to start a project?
+
We need your business details, goals, design preferences, and any existing branding. We'll guide you through our discovery process step by step.
